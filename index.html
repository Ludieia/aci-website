About ACICORP Inc

Serving with dignity, compassion, and responsibility.

America Chaplains International Corp Inc. is an international humanitarian and chaplaincy organization registered in Delaware, USA and operating in Haiti. Its work supports vulnerable children, families, youth, prisoners, migrants, and communities through practical service and spiritual accompaniment.

AMMI — Ayiti Merite Miyò

Ayiti merite diyite, sekirite, jistis, edikasyon, travay, ak espwa.

AMMI — Ayiti Merite Miyò is a civic, humanitarian, and moral commitment promoted by ACICORP Inc. to encourage Haitians and friends of Haiti to work for a country built on dignity, responsibility, unity, discipline, justice, and hope.

Haiti is not only pain; Haiti is resilience, faith, courage, intelligence, and possibility. AMMI calls communities, youth, professionals, churches, institutions, partners, and the diaspora to participate in rebuilding Haiti through concrete action and responsible service.
