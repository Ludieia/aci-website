About ACI

Serving with dignity, compassion, and responsibility.

America Chaplains International Corp Inc. is an international humanitarian and chaplaincy organization registered in Delaware, USA and operating in Haiti. Its work supports vulnerable children, families, youth, prisoners, migrants, and communities through practical service and spiritual accompaniment.

Humanitarian priorities in Haiti

Current priorities include school support, meals for children, educational materials, displaced families, migrants in border areas, vulnerable prisoners, legal and volunteer support, microcredit for small vendors and women entrepreneurs, and improved community access in Grand Gosier.
