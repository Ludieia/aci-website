About America Chaplains International Corp Inc.

Serving Haiti with dignity, compassion, and responsibility.

America Chaplains International Corp Inc. is an international humanitarian and chaplaincy organization registered in Delaware, USA and operating in Haiti through education support, humanitarian response, youth engagement, human rights support, and community recovery initiatives.

Mission

Restore dignity, strengthen communities, and support vulnerable people through humanitarian and chaplaincy action.

Vision

A Haiti where dignity, stability, education, justice, and opportunity are accessible to all communities.

Approach

We combine humanitarian response, spiritual accompaniment, education support, civic responsibility, and community resilience.
