About America Chaplains International Corp Inc.

Serving Haiti with dignity, compassion, and responsibility.

America Chaplains International Corp Inc. is an international humanitarian and chaplaincy organization registered in Delaware, USA and operating in Haiti through education support, humanitarian response, youth engagement, human rights support, and community recovery initiatives.

International identity with Haiti-centered action

ACICORP Inc. was established to respond to human suffering without reducing people to their pain. We serve in a spirit of dignity and responsibility, working alongside communities, local leaders, volunteers, and institutional partners.

Our identity combines humanitarian engagement, chaplaincy care, education support, social recovery, and peace-oriented community action.

  • Registered in Delaware, United States
  • Operational engagement across Haiti’s 10 departments
  • Local offices in Grand Gosier and Port-au-Prince
  • Institutional orientation prepared for collaboration
