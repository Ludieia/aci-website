Humanitarian Response

Dignified action in times of crisis and instability.

ACICORP Inc. provides humanitarian response with a focus on dignity, responsibility, and practical relief for communities facing crisis, instability, and displacement in Haiti. Our approach recognizes urgent needs while also respecting the strength, humanity, and resilience of the people we serve.

We aim to respond in ways that are compassionate, organized, and community-aware. Humanitarian action should not only deliver assistance. It should also preserve dignity, reduce vulnerability, and help communities move toward stability and recovery.

Through local presence, field awareness, and mission-driven coordination, ACICORP Inc. seeks to support families and individuals affected by hardship with seriousness, discipline, and ethical care.

Human Rights

Dignity, responsibility, and humane engagement.

Displaced Families

Dignified support for families facing instability.

ACICORP Inc. stands with displaced families facing instability, uncertainty, and loss of normal community support. Our response is rooted in dignity, compassion, and responsibility, recognizing that displacement affects not only material conditions but also emotional stability, family structure, and human hope.

We seek to accompany families with serious and respectful support, avoiding language that reduces people to crisis alone. Haiti is a country of resilient people, and displaced families deserve attention that protects dignity while responding to urgent needs.

Our work in this area is directed toward practical solidarity, humane presence, and recovery-oriented support that helps families move from disruption toward greater stability.

Chaplaincy Programs

Compassionate presence with ethical care.

ACICORP Inc. develops chaplaincy programs that bring compassionate presence, ethical care, and hope-centered accompaniment to individuals and communities facing hardship. Our chaplaincy identity is spiritual without excess and grounded in dignity, responsibility, and service.

We understand chaplaincy as a ministry of presence that listens, encourages, supports, and stands beside people in difficult circumstances. This work is especially meaningful where vulnerability, trauma, loss, or exclusion can leave people feeling unseen or abandoned.

Our chaplaincy programs are designed to complement humanitarian action by affirming the human person, strengthening moral resilience, and offering compassionate accompaniment in a respectful and credible way.
