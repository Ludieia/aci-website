Programs & Humanitarian Action

Restore dignity. Rebuild hope. Stand with Haiti.

America Chaplains International Corp Inc. develops community-centered, partnership-ready, and Haiti-focused operational program areas shaped by dignity, compassion, responsibility, and credible humanitarian engagement.

Emergency Education Support

Saving the current school year for vulnerable children.

ACICORP Inc. is actively seeking long-term support to help pay teachers in five different schools serving vulnerable communities in Haiti. Many schools face serious financial instability, placing the current academic year at risk for hundreds of children and youth.

Our goal is to establish a stable monthly support base that allows teachers to continue working with dignity while protecting educational continuity for students whose future depends on access to learning.

This initiative is not only about salaries. It is about protecting hope, stability, discipline, and educational opportunity during a period of national fragility.

School Construction Initiative

Building schools for a stronger future.

ACICORP Inc. seeks support for the construction of five schools designed to provide safer, more stable, and more dignified learning environments for children and youth in underserved areas of Haiti.

Many children currently study in fragile conditions that limit concentration, security, and educational progress. This initiative aims to help communities move toward more structured educational opportunities capable of strengthening long-term social development.

By investing in school infrastructure, we invest in future leadership, community resilience, and national renewal.

Human Rights & Prison Justice

Defending dignity for vulnerable detainees.

ACICORP Inc. is developing human rights initiatives focused on supporting vulnerable individuals who remain in prison despite having exceeded their legal detention period or lacking the resources necessary to access fair legal support.

Our approach seeks to encourage responsible advocacy, legal orientation, humanitarian attention, and dignity-centered engagement for individuals who are often forgotten or abandoned within fragile systems.

We believe justice, dignity, and humane treatment must remain central to any serious humanitarian mission.

Food Distribution for Displaced Families

Emergency food support for displaced communities.

ACICORP Inc. seeks to organize structured food distribution programs for displaced families and vulnerable households affected by instability, insecurity, and humanitarian crisis in Haiti.

Many displaced families face severe food insecurity, especially children, elderly individuals, and mothers living in fragile conditions. This initiative aims to provide organized, respectful, and dignity-centered food assistance capable of reducing immediate suffering while strengthening community solidarity.

Our objective is to combine emergency humanitarian response with compassionate and responsible field engagement.

University Support for Youth

Helping young people pursue education instead of delinquency.

ACICORP Inc. believes access to higher education can help protect vulnerable youth from instability, hopelessness, violence, and delinquency. Many talented young people are unable to continue university studies because of financial hardship and lack of opportunity.

This program seeks to provide university assistance, tuition support, mentorship, and educational orientation for young people committed to building a better future for themselves, their families, and their communities.

Supporting youth education is an investment in peace, leadership, stability, and long-term national resilience.

Housing for Displaced Families

Building more than 200 small homes for vulnerable displaced families.

ACICORP Inc. is preparing a large-scale humanitarian housing initiative focused on the construction of more than 200 small homes designed to provide protection, dignity, and greater stability for displaced families living in extremely vulnerable conditions.

Many families currently live without adequate shelter, security, or basic living conditions. This initiative seeks to create safer spaces capable of restoring hope, protecting children, and helping families rebuild their lives with dignity.

Our vision is to combine humanitarian compassion with practical community recovery through organized and responsible action.
