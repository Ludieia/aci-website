Programs & Initiatives

Integrated humanitarian and chaplaincy support programs.

America Chaplains International Corp Inc. develops practical and humanitarian programs that support children, families, youth, vulnerable communities, and community recovery in Haiti.

Chaplaincy

Spiritual accompaniment and compassionate support for vulnerable individuals and communities.

Education Support

School continuity, educational materials, school rehabilitation, and support for children.

Humanitarian Response

Support for displaced families, migrants, and communities affected by instability.

Human Rights

Human dignity advocacy, accompaniment, legal support, and volunteer engagement.

Prison Support

Human-centered support and chaplaincy accompaniment for vulnerable prisoners.

Community Recovery

Cooperative development, local resilience, microcredit support, and recovery initiatives.
