America Chaplains International Corp Inc. develops humanitarian, educational, chaplaincy, and community-centered initiatives designed to strengthen dignity, resilience, stability, and hope across Haiti.

Humanitarian Response

Practical humanitarian action rooted in dignity.

ACICORP Inc. provides humanitarian response with a focus on dignity, responsibility, and practical relief for communities facing crisis, instability, and displacement in Haiti.

We aim to respond in ways that are compassionate, organized, and community-aware while helping communities move toward stability and recovery.

  • Support for vulnerable families facing urgent hardship
  • Response assistance for displaced households
  • Community-based relief grounded in local realities
  • Recovery-oriented support for stability and hope

Education Support

Behind every child, there is a future.

ACICORP Inc. believes education is one of the strongest foundations for dignity, opportunity, and long-term community renewal.

We seek to strengthen educational access and encouragement through practical support, community engagement, and initiatives that protect children and youth at risk of exclusion.

  • Education strengthens confidence and resilience
  • It creates opportunity for youth and communities
  • It supports peace, dignity, and leadership
  • It encourages hope and long-term renewal
