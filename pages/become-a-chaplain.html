Prison Chaplains

Military Chaplains

Pastoral care and ethical support for service members and families.

Community Chaplains

School & University Chaplains

Support for students, educators, values formation, counseling, and youth leadership.

Hospital & Health Chaplains

Compassionate support for patients, families, and caregivers.

Registration Pathway

How the process will work

1. Interest

Submit basic information and choose a chaplaincy pathway.

2. Review

ACICORP Inc. reviews the candidate profile, motivation, and service orientation.

3. Orientation

Candidate receives guidance about ethics, service expectations, and humanitarian responsibility.

4. Training

Training covers chaplaincy, humanitarian service, dignity, crisis response, and community support.

5. Service

Approved chaplains may serve in community, institutional, humanitarian, or volunteer settings.

6. Reporting

Activities will be followed through the future CRM / ERP platform for coordination.
