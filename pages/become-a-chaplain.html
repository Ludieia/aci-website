Become a Chaplain

Serve with compassion, dignity, and spiritual responsibility.

ACICORP Inc. welcomes expressions of interest from people called to serve communities, institutions, families, youth, prisoners, and vulnerable populations with integrity and humanitarian commitment.

Chaplaincy Pathways

Discern the area of service that matches your calling.

Community Chaplaincy

Support families, neighborhoods, and local communities through ethical service, listening, and presence.

Prison Chaplaincy

Serve vulnerable prisoners with dignity-centered accompaniment, moral support, and responsible engagement.

Youth & School Chaplaincy

Accompany youth, schools, and educational environments with care, values, and hope-centered leadership.

Hospital & Health Chaplaincy

Offer compassionate support in health-related settings with seriousness, empathy, and spiritual responsibility.

Humanitarian Chaplaincy

Support vulnerable populations affected by crisis, displacement, and hardship through disciplined service.

Institutional Chaplaincy

Prepare for service in structured settings through orientation, review, and mission-aligned follow-up.

Current Intake Process

Start with an official expression of interest.

To begin, please contact ACICORP Inc. with your full name, location, phone number, background of service, and the chaplaincy area that interests you. The team will review your request and guide you toward the next step.

Step 1

Send your expression of interest by email or phone.

Step 2

Receive orientation, document guidance, and initial review instructions.

Step 3

Proceed to coordinated follow-up for mission readiness, service planning, and further onboarding.
