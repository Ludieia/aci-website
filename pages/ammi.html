AMMI — Ayiti Merite Miyò

Serving with dignity, compassion, and responsibility.

AMMI — Ayiti Merite Miyò is an expression of conviction, dignity, and hope. It reflects ACICORP Inc.'s belief that Haiti deserves more than survival. Haiti deserves stability, opportunity, respect, and credible pathways toward renewal.

A message both human and institutional

This initiative embodies a message that is both human and institutional: Haiti is not only pain; Haiti is resilience, faith, and hope. Through AMMI, we affirm a vision of the country that honors courage, possibility, and the deep value of its people.

Ayiti Merite Miyò is not a slogan of illusion. It is a call to seriousness, responsibility, and shared commitment. It invites communities, partners, and supporters to believe in a better future built with dignity and action.
