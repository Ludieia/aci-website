ACI Chaplain Market

Training, uniforms, materials, and official chaplaincy resources.

The Chaplain Market is being prepared as the official space where candidates, chaplains, volunteers, and partners will access training programs, uniforms, certificates, materials, and approved items for chaplaincy service.

Market Categories

Resources for chaplaincy preparation and service

Training Programs

Chaplaincy orientation, humanitarian chaplaincy, prison chaplaincy, police chaplaincy, youth chaplaincy, leadership, ethics, crisis response, and field service training.

Uniforms

Official chaplain shirts, polos, jackets, caps, badges, identification materials, and field-ready uniform options.

Certificates & Documents

Training certificates, membership documents, identification requests, official letters, and administrative processing.

Books & Study Materials

Manuals, chaplaincy guides, devotionals, humanitarian response resources, and training documents.

Event & Graduation Fees

Seminar participation, graduation preparation, ordination-related events, conferences, and official ceremonies.

Field Service Kits

Basic field support items for chaplains, volunteers, emergency visits, prison support, youth outreach, and community activities.

How It Will Work

Simple, organized, and connected to registration.

1. Register

Start with the ACI registration pathway so the organization can identify your role and service category.

2. Choose Items

After validation, members will be able to select training, uniforms, documents, or service materials from the market.

3. Pay Securely

Payments may be made through the official listed channels, including MonCash, NatCash, or bank transfer.
