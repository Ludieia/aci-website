ACICORP Inc. welcomes collaboration with humanitarian organizations, faith-based institutions, civil society actors, professional networks, foundations, academic partners, and responsible supporters who share a commitment to dignified service in Haiti.

We are building a partnership platform shaped by credibility, field awareness, ethical stewardship, and practical cooperation. Our purpose is not only to seek support, but to build serious relationships capable of creating meaningful and responsible impact.
